Frequently asked questions

I’m a complete beginner — is this for me?

Especially for you. Most members arrive with little or no gym experience. Everything starts with a movement screen and a goal-setting chat, and every exercise is coached from the ground up. You will never be handed a workout and left to it.

Is the studio really private?

Yes. It’s a dedicated private studio in Marston Moretaine, Bedford. During your session it’s just you (or your small group) and Kelly — no strangers, no cameras-out crowds, no waiting for equipment.

What if I have a health condition or injury?

Kelly regularly coaches women through post-natal recovery, PCOS, menopause, joint issues and returns from injury. Your programme is built around your body — and she’ll tell you honestly if something needs a GP or physio first.

Do I have to follow a strict diet?

No. Nutrition support is optional and it’s never a rigid meal plan. As an MNU-certified nutritionist, Kelly works with flexible, evidence-based guidance that fits your real life — including takeaway Fridays.

What happens after the 30-day trial?

You choose. Continue with group training, move to 1:1 or 2:1 coaching, or simply finish your month with no obligation. There’s no long contract and no pressure — the results do the persuading.

Where are you based?

The studio is in Marston Moretaine, Bedford — easy to reach from Bedford, Kempston, Wootton, Cranfield and the surrounding villages, with free parking right outside.
